Who We Are

Bringing Hope to Those Who Need It Most

Why Zari Foundation started: Zari Foundation was started with a simple belief no one should have to navigate life’s challenges alone. The foundation was initially created with a focus on supporting and empowering women through awareness, community, and meaningful conversations.

Over time, this vision expanded to include children, the elderly, and animals recognizing that care, compassion, and support should extend to every life that needs it. Zari Foundation aims to build a space where empathy meets action, and where every individual feels valued, supported, and uplifted.

Our Mission

To empower women by creating awareness and fostering supportive communities, while extending compassion and care to children, the elderly, and animals in need.

Our Vision

A world where every life especially women, children, the elderly, and animals is supported, valued, and empowered to live with dignity and care.

Our Focus Areas

Where We Direct Our Efforts

We believe in a holistic approach to community development, focusing on these key pillars.

Women Empowerment

Providing skill development, financial literacy, and support systems to help women achieve independence and confidence.

Child Education

Ensuring every child has access to quality education, learning materials, and environments that foster growth.

Elderly Care

Offering companionship, healthcare support, and safe environments for our senior citizens to age with dignity.

Health & Awareness

Conducting medical camps, hygiene workshops, and mental health awareness programs in underserved areas.

Community Development

Building sustainable infrastructure and fostering community bonds through localized, collaborative initiatives.

Animal Care

Providing food, medical assistance, and safe shelter to stray and abandoned animals in our communities.

What We Do

Turning Compassion Into Action

  • Education Support Programs

    Sponsoring tuition, providing textbooks, and running after-school tutoring.

  • Donation Drives

    Collecting and distributing clothes, food, and daily essentials year-round.

  • Volunteer Programs

    Mobilizing passionate individuals to create direct impact on the ground.

  • Health Camps

    Organizing free check-ups, eye clinics, and distributing essential medicines.

  • Skill Development Workshops

    Vocational training for adults to secure better livelihood opportunities.
